ABOUT OUR COMMUNITY

Founded on the pursuit of self-discovery, Saint Paul Preparatory Seoul (SPPS) engages students in a challenging college prepatory education. SPPS was established in 2008 as non-profit, independent, co-educational private school. The school has offered and continues to offer advanced curricula in from elementary to high school.
